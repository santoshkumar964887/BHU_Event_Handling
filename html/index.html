About Us

Yugaantar , the Annual Cultural Festival of Department of Computer Science,BHU, is a majestic three days cultural fest, recognised as one of the greatest pomp youth festivals of India. Yugaantar encompasses lavish history , splendid culture and a profoundly rich heritage of CS, BHU. Recognising this event of gigantic proportions , the Government proclaimed Yugaantar as the "Annual Youth Festival Of Varanasi " in 2005. Yugaantar has acquired the credentials of being the largest cultural fest of Northern India by playing host to a multitude of talented scholars and media moguls in an annual celebration of culture and intellect. Thomso now celebrates its 36th year with this year's theme being "Siezed by Stardust".



Why Yugaantar?

Yugaantar is the largest cultural festivals of North India attracting a crowd of over 30000 people. With over 150+ events in line, Yugaantar is a three day cultural extravaganza playing host to eminent celebrities and talented scholars.

New Vishwanath Temple, BHU

Shri Vishwanath Mandir is situated inside the campus of Benaras Hindu University about 9 kilometers South of Varanasi Railway Station.The old vishwanath temple is about 7 kilometer from this temple. The temple is located at a serene and peaceful location in a vast campus of Banaras Hindu University. It does not have large crowd and chaos as in the old vishwanath temple. There are proper crowd management at the place. Orderly queues, vast shoe keeping store, proper place for hand washing etc. are well managed.
